Sabeto Hot Springs and Mud Pool

Discover one of Fiji's most unique natural attractions at the Sabeto Hot Springs and Mud Pool, located just a short drive from Nadi. Tucked at the base of the Sleeping Giant mountain range, this hidden gem offers a blend of fun, relaxation, and traditional Fijian wellness. Start by covering yourself in warm, mineral-rich volcanic mud, said to nourish the skin and ease tension. Once it dries, rinse off and soak in a series of natural geothermal pools, each with slightly different temperatures that soothe muscles and rejuvenate the body.

For the ultimate experience, enjoy a traditional Fijian massage afterward while taking in the peaceful surroundings. The site includes changing rooms, restrooms, and small local stalls selling souvenirs and crafts. Sabeto also serves as a perfect gateway to nearby cultural sites like local villages and the Garden of the Sleeping Giant. Whether you're coming for a quick soak or planning a relaxing day out, the Sabeto Hot Springs and Mud Pool is a must-visit for anyone seeking Fiji's natural side.
