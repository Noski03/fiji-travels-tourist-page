Fiji's only zoo

Kula Wild Adventure Park

Kula WILD Adventure Park covers about 28 acres across three valleys, with some 12 acres of coastal forest open to visitors. Visitors can stroll through large walk-through enclosures where parrots, doves, water birds, fruit bats, iguanas and reptiles live in settings that mimic their natural habitats. The marine displays include ocean turtles, soft and hard corals, and tropical fish, offering a look at the vibrant reef life of Fiji. The park features a 110-metre Jungle Water Slide that twists down a hillside and crosses a river, plus a Kiddies Splash Pool with slides, water jets and splash features.

One of the standout attractions is the “Kula Krazy Canopy Flier,” a canopy zip-rail (often described as a roller-coaster style zip line) unique in Fiji. There's almost a kilometre of elevated wooden walkways, bridges and rest stops, well-signposted displays, and staff ready to help and answer questions. Animal interaction opportunities include holding iguanas or snakes, and feeding young Hawksbill sea turtles three times daily at set times.
