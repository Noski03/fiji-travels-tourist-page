ATV Quad Bike Adventure Tour

This quad-bike adventure tour takes you to places in Fiji that are too difficult to reach on foot or without an off-roading vehicle. Save time traveling by a speedy quad bike with a guide navigating the way, and ride up toward the Nakala Mountains for stunning views of the surrounding area. Plus, bottled water is included along the way to keep you hydrated in the heat.
