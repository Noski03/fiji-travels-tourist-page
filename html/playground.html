Playgrounds for kids

The best activities for a family with kids

Kula WILD Adventure Park (Coral Coast, Viti Levu) A fun family park with parrots, iguanas, turtles, rainforest walkways, a jungle waterslide, and educational exhibits. Good for kids of all ages.

Big Bula Waterpark (Denarau Island, near Nadi) A water park with inflatable slides and rides. There's a separate area for smaller children to play safely.

Sigatoka River Safari: A fun day trip with jet boat rides, river cruising, and visits to local Fijian villages.

Ecotrax Fiji (Coral Coast) Ride electric-assisted bikes on old sugar-cane tracks, enjoy countryside views, and finish at a beach for a swim. Good for families.
