Surfing in Fiji

Fiji is a world-renowned surfing destination, offering a wide variety of waves across its thousands of islands and coral reefs. Its islands face every direction of the Pacific Ocean, ensuring consistent swells year-round, from heavy reef breaks to mellow beach waves.

Surfing in Fiji is generally divided into three main regions: the Mamanucas, home to legendary waves like Cloudbreak and Restaurants; the Coral Coast, with accessible reef breaks and a laid-back tropical vibe; and Taveuni, the “Garden Island,” offering remote, uncrowded surf experiences in pristine surroundings.

Beyond surfing, Fiji's warm waters, vibrant marine life, and welcoming culture make every trip memorable. Between sessions, you can snorkel over coral gardens, explore jungles, or relax under palm trees with a fresh coconut. With year-round waves, incredible scenery, and authentic island spirit, Fiji is a top surf destination.
