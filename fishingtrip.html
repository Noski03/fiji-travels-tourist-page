The best fishing trips in Fiji

Sigatoka Fishing Charters, is located on the South of Fiji's main island called Viti Levu. With Sigatoka Fishing Charters, traveling anglers have the opportunity to experience these beautiful surroundings from the water, and rarely does the fishing disappoint. Trolling lures is the most common tactic which results in the usual suspects: tuna, marlin, sailfish, wahoo, and mahi. It is the Coral Coast after all, so bottom fishing can also be productive over the reefs just offshore, and the legendary Giant Trevally (GTs) are always around. The boat is a 28 ft Aluminum Catamaran and has the basic necessities needed for a great day on the water. Free transfers are available from all resorts in Denarau and the Coral Coast. Follow Sigatoka Fishing Charters Facebook page to find out what we have been catching lately.

Just 10 minutes from Nadi Town, Malolo Fisher sports is located in Kiosk 5 at Port Denarau Terminal , Denarau. We offer our guests some of the greatest game fishing adventures and charters in Fiji. Our fleet of modern, fully equipped custom designed game fishing vessels have a capacity to host up to 25 passengers. Why Go Fishing With Us? We are 100% Fijian owned and our local village boys who have the expert information on the best fishing locations in the Mamanucas and Yasawas. Our fishing charters can be customised to your needs be it sports fishing , deep sea fishing, GT popping and Jigging or bottom fishing. You can be sure we use top class Penn, Tiagra, Shimano and Stella fishing gears; Pakula, Zacc Atak, Williamsons, Rapala, and Halco Lures in all our fishing charters.
